I’m Thijmen, an ecologically friendly handyman in essence. Working mainly with wood and steel, I do both large and small projects, purely for aesthetics, for fun or for things like environmental activism. I construct houses, build exhibitions, or make my own autonomous creations. Some might describe me as an enthusiastic, slightly workaholic non-stop talking tall man with a big smile and a laugh you can hear from miles away. Which, for better or for worse is quite fitting.
